Privacy Policy for Pixel Galaxy Hunter

This app, Pixel Galaxy Hunter, can store gameplay progress on the device and can send leaderboard data online when the global leaderboard is enabled.

Data used by the app

  • Display name entered by the player for leaderboard use
  • Gameplay statistics such as score and best wave
  • A locally generated player identifier used to update leaderboard entries
  • Basic app settings stored on the device

Purpose

  • Show local progress and saved settings
  • Submit and load online leaderboard entries
  • Display high scores inside the game

What the app does not do

  • No account registration
  • No in-app purchases
  • No direct payment processing
  • No advertising SDK is included in the current build

Storage

  • Local progress is stored on the device
  • Online leaderboard entries are stored in the configured backend service
